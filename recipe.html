Beans Porridge Recipe

A nutritious tasty nigerian dish made with beans, palm oil (red oil) and spices.

Recipe Info

prep time: 20 minutes

cook time 1 hour

Serving 4 People

Difficulty Beginner

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of beans
  • 1/2 cup of palm oil
  • 1 large onion (diced or chopped)
  • 2 tablespoon of grounded crayfish
  • 4 seasoning cubes
  • 1 table spoon of salt
  • 1 table spoon of grounded pepper

Instruction

  1. Wash the beans thoroughly
  2. Put the beans in a pot add water boil until it softens
  3. Add your already diced or chopped onions, grounded crayfish, pepper and 4 seasoning cubes
  4. Stir well and allow to cook on low heat
  5. Simmer well until the beans are thick and porridge like

Tips

you can add yam or plantain to make it taste even richer
